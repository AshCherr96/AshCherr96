Reelfoot Lake State Park

Reelfoot Lake State Park, located in the northwest corner of the state, is home to an amazing assortment of wildlife. The area features fantastic fishing and wildlife viewing opportunities!

history

Reelfoot Lake was formed by the New Madrid earthquakes of 1811-1812. The area has a fascinating history!

things to do

  • Hike
  • Fish
  • Birdwatch
  • Wildlife watch
  • Camp
  • Picnic

places to stay

There are a variety of options! Enjoy camping at one of the two state campgrounds, stay in the state park cabins, rent a hotel room, or consider renting a privately-owned cabin!

dining

There are multiple restaurant options in the area. Feast on catfish at several local restaurants, or try bbq! There's also an upscale restaurant available.
