Vincent Price's Published Works

During his lifetime, Vincent Price published several literary works about topics that he was passionate about: Cooking and Art! He also published his autobiography and other books giving a glimpse into his life.

A List of Vincent's Literary Works

  • I Like What I Know - A Visual Autobiography (1959)
  • The book of Joe; about a dog and his man (1961)
  • A Treasury of Great Recipes (1965) - *His wife, Mary Grant, co-authored this with him.*
  • Mary and Vincent Price Present A National Treasury of Cookery (1967) - *Mary Grant was the co-author.*
  • Come Into the Kitchen Cook Book: A Collector's Treasury of America's Great Recipes (1969) - *Mary Grant was also the co-author.*
  • Cooking Price-wise with Vincent Price (1971)
  • The Vincent Price Treasury of American Art (1972)
  • Vincent Price: His Movies, His Plays, His Life (1978)
